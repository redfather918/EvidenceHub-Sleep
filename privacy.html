Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

1. Introduction

EvidenceHub Sleep ("we", "our", or "the Service") is an open-source evidence-based sleep knowledge system that automates the creation and distribution of educational video content to social media platforms including TikTok. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

The Service is designed as a self-hosted automation tool. When you run the Service on your own infrastructure:

  • Video Content: You provide your own video files for upload. These are sent directly to TikTok's API servers and are not stored on our systems.
  • API Credentials: OAuth tokens (TikTok refresh tokens) are stored locally in environment configuration files (e.g., .env) on your own machine or server. We do not collect, transmit, or store these credentials on any third-party server.
  • No Usage Tracking: The Service does not include analytics, tracking pixels, or any form of usage telemetry.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Upload videos you explicitly select to your connected TikTok account via TikTok's official Content Posting API.
  • Refresh OAuth tokens to maintain API access (tokens are exchanged directly with TikTok's authentication servers).

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Video content you choose to upload is shared exclusively with TikTok's platform APIs for publishing purposes.

5. Data Security

Since EvidenceHub Sleep is a self-hosted open-source tool:

  • API credentials remain under your control in your local environment.
  • We recommend storing credentials in secure environment variables and restricting file access permissions.
  • We do not operate any central server that processes or stores user data.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

This Privacy Policy is published in conjunction with the EvidenceHub Sleep open-source project hosted at github.com/redfather918/EvidenceHub-Sleep.
